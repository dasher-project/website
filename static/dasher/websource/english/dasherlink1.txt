 &lt;p&gt;&lt;b&gt;Dasher&lt;/b&gt; is a truly innovative free software package that
 allows the user to write efficiently, without a keyboard, by
 simple steering gestures. &lt;a 
 href="http://www.inference.phy.cam.ac.uk/dasher/"&gt;&lt;img alt="Writing
 hello with Dasher" align=right width="96" height="82" style="border:0"
  src=
"http://www.inference.phy.cam.ac.uk/dasher/images/mouse96.gif"
 &gt;&lt;/a&gt;
 Steering with an ordinary mouse, beginners can write at 25 words
 per minute after an hour of practice. Dasher can be used equally
 easily with touchscreens, head-mice, or eyetrackers.  It's useful
 for people with special needs, for children, and for anyone who
 wants an alternative to a keyboard.  With Dasher, you can write
 in almost any language. Dasher is available for all the major
 personal computer platforms: GNU/Linux, Windows, and MacOS-X.
 It's free software, and will always be free.  &lt;/p&gt;
 &lt;p&gt; Dasher is fast, efficient, easy to learn, and fun to use.

 beginners, download Dasher, and steer away!&lt;/p&gt;
