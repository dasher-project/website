HiddenPage pocketPC tips

Additional tips for the pocketPC version of Dasher Tue 22/1/02

  • There are three special strongly-coloured squares in this version of Dasher, coloured orange-ish, red, and green; all three squares do not produce any character; these squares indicate sub-groupings of the alphabet. (Uppercase Alphabet, Numeric characters, and Punctuation.)
